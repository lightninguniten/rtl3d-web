Lightning in Malaysia

Everything you need for the lightning-safety quiz.

🌍
100flashes / second

Lightning strikes Earth about 100 times every second — roughly 8 million bolts a day.

🔥
30,000°Chotter than the Sun's surface

A bolt heats the air to about 30,000 °C — 5× hotter than the Sun's surface. That blast of expanding air is what we hear as thunder.

KLANG VALLEY
Storm days per year
~240

The Klang Valley around Subang Jaya is one of the most lightning-prone places on Earth — about 240 thunderstorm days a year.

RTL3D
Real-Time Lightning 3D Imaging & Forecasting

A Malaysia–Japan (SATREPS) research project that images lightning live — to protect people and the power grid.

📡
LF + VHFradio antennas → 3D images

RTL3D uses LF sensors to catch the main strokes and VHF sensors to map the whole channel — building lightning in 3D.

⚡🔊
Flash → Banghow far away is the strike?

Count the seconds from flash to thunder, then ÷ 3 for km (÷ 5 for miles). Under 30 s? It's already dangerously close.

⏱️
30 – 30the rule that keeps you safe

Thunder within 30 seconds of the flash → go inside. Then wait 30 minutes after the last thunder before heading back out.

🏠
Go Indoors"When thunder roars, go indoors"

There is no safe place outside. Shelter in a fully enclosed building or a hard-top car (a Faraday cage). The old "lightning crouch" was dropped in 2008 — it does not protect you.

⚠️
Avoid these in a storm
  • ✕Tall trees, open fields, hilltops & water
  • ✕Golf clubs, umbrellas — anything that raises your height
  • ✕Showers, taps & corded phones — lightning travels through pipes & wiring
